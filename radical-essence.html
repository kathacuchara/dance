Radical Essence

In this intensive, contemporary dance practice we, Katha and Peter, are inviting you and your bodies to learn with us about how to loosen up stiffnes, stuckness and heavyness and free up blocked and supressed energy from within, using the movement as our main tool. The principle is to soften up and transform the body's tensions into available, genuine movement.

We propose solo/duo/trio and group exercises of improvisation, massage and bodywork, all aimed at understanding and dismantling specific patterns of blocks in the body. Undoing these blocks we allow the body to open up and express itself in loving, authentic ways and find it's pleasure and ever expanding freedom.

Our work is based on contemporary dance and Técnica Presence, along with tools from physical theater, massage, and Butoh dance. More than a physical training, this workshop is a practice of reconnection with the living presence of the body, oriented towards the individual movement language of each person and thus the unfolding of a genuinely embodied presence in our everyday moving and dancing.
