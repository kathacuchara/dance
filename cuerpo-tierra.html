Cuerpo Tierra

The CUERPO.TIERRA project emerges from the need to explore and implement forward-oriented strategies that rethink and, above all, reimagine how to live the relationships we have (learned) with our bodies and with ourselves, with each other and with the Earth.

First things first: the most pressing task was (and still is!) the pruning of the old olive trees (Empeltre variety). They have not been pruned for over 6 years and have grown a majestic natural crown. We decided to take out some branches and observe their development over the year. Also, almond trees have been blooming. As it has been quite a water-rich year, we are hoping to get a good olive and almond harvest!

In February 2026 we finally dared to do it: to become "stewardesses" of 4 ha of land - a beautiful olive and almond grove in the Region of Terra Alta, Catalonia. Here, we will learn from and with nature: how to observe, how to adapt our rhythms to her rhythms, how to communicate and respond to her signals. How to interact, to create, to be in exchange, to take and to give back. We are looking forward!
